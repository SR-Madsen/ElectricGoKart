ME1117

Item# ME1117

Product Description

Designed for long life. No brush maintenance. The motor is 90% efficient at voltages between 17 to 50VAC. Continuous current of 120 amps. This is a 3-phase, Y-connected Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor with an axial air gap.

1) This is a 8 pole motor (4 pole pairs). 2) The Phase to Phase winding resistance is 0.013 Ohms. 3) The maximum recommended rotor speed is 5000 RPM. 4) Voltages from 0 to 72 VDC input to the control. 5) Torque constant of 0.13 Nm per Amp 6) The Inductance Phase to Phase is 0.10 Milli-Henry. 7) Armature Inertia is 52 Kg Cm Squared. 8) Continuous current of 80 Amps AC. 9) Peak current of 220 Amps AC for 1 minute. 10) Weight of 23 pounds. 11) Peak Stall Torque if 38 Nm. 11) This is an Open Frame, Fan Cooled motor. 12) Has internal temperature sensor.
